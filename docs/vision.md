# FinPilot AI Vision

## Mission
Empower students and young professionals to build healthy financial habits through AI-powered budgeting, spending insights, and financial education.

## Target Users
- College students
- University students
- International students living alone
- Young professionals

## Core Problem
Young adults often overspend because they lack simple tools to understand and plan their finances.

## Core Values
- Clarity
- Discipline
- Privacy
